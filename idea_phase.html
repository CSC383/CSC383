Idea Phase

In the Idea Phase, your business hasn't yet taken shape. Maybe all you have is an idea, or just the motivation to start something special. At this point you probably have a lot of questions, and you may be unsure about things. Don't Worry! We're here to help. This is a scary but exciting time. We're happy to help you through it and acheive your goals.

Using the resources that are available to you is key to your success. We'd like to connect you with all the resources necessary to start your business. Whether you need advice, finances, or legal help, we have you covered. Below is a list of each class of resource, and a link to their respective sites.

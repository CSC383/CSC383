About Us

The Innovation Incubator is a program run by U of M Flint's University Outreach team to help local entrepreneurs develop their companies. Whether you're already in business or just have a great idea, we can help you.

We are both a co-working space and a business incubator dedicated to helping businesses and non-profits start up. The Innovation Incubator is the only incubator in Flint focused on (but not limited to) social entrepreneurship, innovation, and creative economy-based ventures.

Services

  • Co-work and meeting space for up to 24 people
  • Free wi-fi access
  • White boards, projector and projector screen available
  • Business reference library and social enterprise periodicals
  • Business planning assistance
  • Networking opportunities
  • Free business development workshops
